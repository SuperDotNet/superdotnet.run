The Gold Box

If you do not provide a claim, I will create a prayer and place it in The Gold Box, of which you will get the first claim for that work. I have been getting into the habit of having a daily prayer/blessing lately, to sort of work on my writing skills but also to do my part in adding a little good into the world.

As I describe in my about, I am not praying to a specific, defined entity, but more to the “Unknown,” if that can be said. If it helps, it could be said that I am praying to the “thing” that is responsible for all the bizarre discoveries with quantum mechanics and physics we see in the world.

Einstein himself called quantum mechanics spooky action at a distance, and let’s just say that I am in a bit of agreement with him as of late. As such, I am doing the most natural action in response to it as it occurs to me: praying to (and for) it.
